Getting Started

How do I create my first habit?

To create your first habit, tap the "+" button in the top right corner of the Habits screen. You'll be able to:

  • Name your habit
  • Choose an icon that represents it
  • Set how often you want to do it (daily, weekly, etc.)
  • Add optional notes or reminders

How do I mark a habit as complete?

Simply tap on the habit in your list to mark it as complete. You'll see a satisfying confetti animation to celebrate your achievement!

Pro Tip: You can also swipe right on a habit to mark it as complete, or swipe left to delete it.

Managing Your Habits

Can I edit past habits?

No, for data integrity purposes, you cannot edit habits from previous days. You can only edit habits for the current day and future days.

How do I delete a habit?

Long press on the habit you want to delete, then select "Delete" from the context menu that appears.

Can I change the frequency of my habits?

Yes! Tap on any habit to edit it, and you can modify its frequency, name, or icon at any time.

How do I organize my habits?

You can reorder your habits by long-pressing and dragging them to your preferred position. This helps you prioritize your most important habits.

Data & Privacy

What happens to my data if I delete the app?

Your data is stored securely in the cloud. If you reinstall the app and sign in with the same account, your data will be restored.

How is my data protected?

We take your privacy seriously. All your data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal information with third parties.

Can I export my habit data?

Currently, we don't offer data export functionality, but we're working on adding this feature in a future update.

Troubleshooting

The app is not syncing my data

Try these steps:

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Sign out and sign back in
  3. Force close and reopen the app
  4. If the problem persists, contact support

I'm not receiving notifications

Make sure you have:

  1. Enabled notifications for Donezo in your device settings
  2. Set up reminder times for your habits
  3. Allowed background app refresh
